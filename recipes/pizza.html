Pizza

This is a Pizza ! an italian famous food that is very delicious and healthy

to make it you just need four ingredients !

Ingredients :

  • pizza dough
  • tomato sauce
  • cheese
  • Salt

Steps :

  1. roll the dough on the table
  2. add tomato sauce and spread it evenly
  3. add some cheese
  4. put it an the oven for 30 minutes and you'r ready !
