Shawarma

This is Shawarma ! a middle-eastern famous food that is very delicious and healthy

to make it you just need four ingredients !

Ingredients :

  • bread
  • White or red meat
  • mayo or Ketchup
  • Pickles

Steps :

  1. grill the bread on the fire a bit
  2. put the meat inside the bread
  3. put the type of salsa you want
  4. add some pickles then wrap it up !
